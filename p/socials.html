Social media

Hey,

This is not Twitter, you say? You are right.

When Twitter stopped feeling like home; that is, a place to connect with like-minded people and explore niches of fascination, I decided to take an extended break from social media. My old Twitter account was archived, deactivated, and eventually deleted.

But after a few years of inactivity, I decided to come back with the goal of fighting the algorithm from within.

I am starting anew, with a clean slate, approaching social media with the following goals in mind:

  • Be an agent of unity;
  • Short-form algorithmic content is rotting our minds—avoid it at all costs;
  • Encourage people to touch grass;
  • Share long-form content;
  • Ask and encourage genuine, thought-provoking questions; and
  • Do not use the "For you" page—this is where discontent resides.

With that said, here is my new Twitter account: @nikpanaousis

You can also follow me on Substack or email me.
